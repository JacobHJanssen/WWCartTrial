Version 2.0 — Patch Notes
🔐 Security & Access
  • Fixed a bug where the ACTIVE tab could be opened without entering the PIN.
  • Migrated data storage from local device storage to a live cloud database (Firebase), so all devices stay in sync in real time.
  • Added manager login (email + password) required before the app loads any data.
  • Added two front desk logins (iPad 1 / iPad 2) and three manager logins (Sonya, Jake, Nikki), each with appropriate permissions.
  • Restricted database access so only logged-in accounts can read or write data (Firestore security rules).
  • Masked phone numbers and driver's license numbers for front desk accounts — front desk can re-enter a new number but never sees the existing one; managers see full details.
  • Front desk accounts can no longer download the golfer database, create backups, restore backups, or delete golfer profiles — manager-only actions.
  • Login sessions expire when the browser/tab is closed, requiring login again next time.
🛺 Cart Checkout
  • Added a clear on-screen error message when attempting to check out a cart number that's already active, instead of silently blocking the button.
  • Every checkout now records which station (iPad 1, iPad 2, or manager login) processed it, shown as a colored badge on ACTIVE and HISTORY for managers.
  • Added a live daily counter on the HISTORY tab showing total cart rentals for the day in 9-hole equivalents (an 18-hole rental counts as 2, a 27-hole as 3, etc.), with a breakdown by hole count. Resets automatically at End of Day.
✍️ Rental Agreement & Consent
  • Rewrote the rental agreement with more detail, including licensing/age requirements, no outside alcohol, impaired operation, scope of use, damage liability, assumption of risk, and indemnification — referencing relevant North Dakota statutes for context.
  • The agreement modal now requires scrolling to the bottom before "I Agree" can be clicked.
  • Each golfer's agreement acceptance is now logged with their name, timestamp, agreement version, and which station processed it.
  • Added a button to download a formatted PDF copy of the current rental agreement.
🧑‍💼 Admin Tab
  • Added a new ADMIN tab, visible only to manager logins.
  • Organized the Admin tab into separate pages (Agreement, Consent Records, Login Activity, Patch Notes) instead of one long scrolling list.
  • Consent Records and Login Activity are grouped by date into expandable sections, with a date picker to search for a specific day.
  • Login Activity logs each manager login's approximate IP address and location for anomaly detection.
👥 Golfer Database
  • Golfer list is now sorted alphabetically and grouped under letter headers instead of one long list.
  • Added a live counter showing the total number of golfers in the database.

Golf Cart Rental Agreement & Liability Waiver

This Agreement is entered into between the undersigned renter ("Operator") and Wildwood Country Club ("the Club"), located in Burlington, North Dakota, in connection with the rental of a golf cart for use on Club premises.

1. Operator Eligibility & Licensing

  • Valid License Required: The Operator must possess a valid driver's license and be at least sixteen (16) years of age, consistent with North Dakota's general minimum age for vehicle operation.
  • No Outside Alcohol: The Operator agrees not to bring outside alcohol onto Club premises. Alcoholic beverages are available for purchase at the clubhouse in accordance with Club policy and applicable law.
  • No Impaired Operation: The Operator shall not operate the cart while impaired by alcohol or any controlled substance to a degree that affects safe operation. The Club reserves the right to immediately suspend rental privileges and refuse further use of Club property for any violation of this provision.
  • Familiarization: It is the sole responsibility of the Operator to become familiar with the safe operation of the cart prior to use, including its controls, braking distance, and stability characteristics.

2. Scope of Use & Operational Rules

  • Course Use Only: This cart is rented for use exclusively on Wildwood Country Club premises and designated cart paths. It is not licensed, titled, or equipped for operation on public roads, and the Operator agrees not to operate it off Club property.
  • Safe & Courteous Operation: Carts must be operated in a safe, careful, and courteous manner at all times, in accordance with posted Club rules and staff instructions.
  • Designated Paths: Carts must remain on designated paths and roadways, and must not be driven on greens, tee boxes, or other areas marked as restricted.
  • Passenger Limit: No more than two (2) persons may occupy the cart at any time, and all occupants must remain seated while the cart is in motion.
  • Key Return: The Operator agrees to return the cart key to the clubhouse promptly upon completion of the round.

3. Liability for Damage

The Operator accepts full responsibility for the cart for the duration of the rental period, beginning at checkout and ending upon the cart's return and inspection by Club staff. The Operator agrees to reimburse the Club for the reasonable cost of repair or replacement for any damage to the cart occurring during the rental period, except for damage resulting solely from a pre-existing defect not disclosed at checkout.

4. Assumption of Risk

The operation of a golf cart, like other recreational and motorized activities, involves inherent risks, including but not limited to tipping, collision, uneven terrain, and weather conditions. By signing below, the Operator acknowledges this Agreement and voluntarily assumes all known and unknown risks associated with the cart's use, to the fullest extent permitted under North Dakota law, including the comparative fault principles set forth in N.D.C.C. Chapter 32-03.2.

5. Release & Indemnification

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Operator releases, indemnifies, and agrees to hold harmless Wildwood Country Club, its owners, officers, employees, and agents from any and all claims, demands, actions, damages, losses, or expenses (including reasonable attorney's fees) arising out of or related to the Operator's use of the cart, except to the extent such claims arise from the Club's own gross negligence or willful misconduct.

6. Acknowledgment

By checking the box and providing your name below, you acknowledge that you have read, scrolled through, and understood this Agreement in its entirety, and that you are entering into it voluntarily as a condition of renting a cart from Wildwood Country Club.

This Agreement does not constitute a comprehensive statement of North Dakota law and is not a substitute for legal advice. Wildwood Country Club recommends periodic legal review of this Agreement.
